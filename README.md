## Why This Matters to SaaS Founders

Running a SaaS business is challenging:  

- Where is your revenue really coming from?  
- Why are users churning?  
- Which product features drive growth?  
- How do you forecast and optimize Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)?  

The **SaaS Growth Intelligence System** answers these questions with **clean, reliable data and intuitive dashboards**, enabling founders to **make confident, data-driven decisions**.
